What does this mod do?

Improved Anvil EE is a mod for BG2 and TOB. It requires Throne of Bhaal or BG2: Enhanced Edition.

  1. Tactical challenges and improved enemies.
  2. Many new quests and encounters.
  3. New types of monsters.
  4. Fixes and tweaks: Improved Anvil applies numerous corrections and changes (including fixes and tweaks) to the original game.
  5. Item Randomizer program: a novelty introduced by Improved Anvil. A good number of powerful items have been removed from their original locations. Each time you start a new game with Improved Anvil, those items will be randomly (and sensibly) redistributed into new and difficult locations and/or in the inventory of improved and tough enemies. In other words, each time you play the game with this mod, each of those items may be in different places and you won't be sure where to look for them.
  6. New item upgrades: Cromwell, Maheer and Cespenar have 100+ new item upgrade recipes most of which require brand new components which are also added to the game by this mod.
  7. Five new kits: two new ranger kits, a new druid kit and a two new fighter kits are added to the game. All new kits are available to the protagonist at character creation stage. In addition, the new druid kit is assigned to Cernd, one of the new ranger kits is assigned to Valygar and one of the fighter kits is assigned to Mazzy Fentan.

All those experienced players who are tired of playing the same game over and over can now play BG2 as if they are playing an entirely new game.

Warnings and important notes

Note 1: Improved Anvil is "the mod for the experienced players". It includes some really difficult battles. If you do not have the required level of tactical knowledge, you will need to have patience and draw a learning curve for yourself and improve your skills gradually while playing the mod. If you don't have the time or the patience for that, you may need to postpone playing this mod to a later time.

Note 2: Never cheat during the game if you intend to play Improved Anvil. Cheating may look harmless at the first glance and may even help you win some battles, but many global variables are checking the events during the game and cheating may mess up the future events. Cheating is not confined to using console commands or using external programs. Any action which is deliberately performed to exploit the game's engine is a cheat. Any action which a DM would not allow you to perform in a PnP is a cheat as well.

Note 3: It is strongly recommended to play Improved Anvil with as many party members as possible. Improved Anvil is not designed for solo characters or even for parties with fewer than five members. Take your time to choose and collect your desired NPCs normally (no need to hurry) in the game, but don't go too far into the game with a party of fewer than 5 members. A full party of 6 members is strongly recommended even over a party of 5 members.

Note 4: If you are a completely new player and appear to be at loss regarding what to do and how to approach the mod, please refer to our small guide for the new players. And don't hesitate to ask a question at our forum!

Version history

For detailed overview, please see the complete version history.

Version 6.2
  1. WeiDU compiler is updated to version 239.
  2. Mod's structure is modernized and improved: this is the first of many steps aimed towards more flexible and modular composition.
  3. A brand-new readme designed from scratch in place of the old collection of text documents.
  4. An optional component that prevents NPCs from leaving due to low reputation is introduced.
  5. More bugfixes and tweaks introduced, both for vanilla game and BG2EE.
  6. Many Bioware NPCs (Jaheira, Minsc, Korgan, Jan, Mazzy, Edwin, Aerie) are tweaked to become more attractive as choices for party members.
  7. Major content changes and additions:
    • the Avenger kit is completely redesigned and assigned to Jaheira;
    • a new fighter kit, Champion of Arvoreen, is added and assigned to Mazzy;
    • a new druidic spell is added, complete with custom-made new animations;
    • a small tactical encounter is added to spice up the early stages of the game;
    • a major new encounter, Improved Demogorgon, is added to provide a satisfied finale to the Watcher's Keep adventure;
    • Necromancer's brand new spell, Recast Vital Energy, is toned down to stimulate a more balanced and diverse spell book configuration;
    • a lot of new graphics added to the game: creature animations and custom colouring, spell animations, portrait icons, new item graphics and so on;
  8. Major item-related changes and additions:
    • expanded randomizer program: four more items are added to random locations;
    • a couple of items moved to the new static locations, as well;
    • a third craftsman is introduced to provide services for the player's party;
    • 10 brand new item upgrades are added to the game;
    • various tweaks are introduced to existing in-game items;
    • a couple of existing items are tweaked to be equippable by the druid characters;
    • magical throwing axes and hammers can now be dual-wielded when in melee mode;
    • efforts made, both through item upgrades and item placement, to make scimitars, katanas and spears more attractive weapon choices.
