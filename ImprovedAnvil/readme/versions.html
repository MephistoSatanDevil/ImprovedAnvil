Version history

Version 6.2
  1. WeiDU compiler is updated to version 239.
  2. Mod's structure is modernized and improved: this is the first of many steps aimed towards more flexible and modular composition.
  3. A brand-new readme designed from scratch in place of the old collection of text documents.
  4. More bugfixes and tweaks introduced, both for vanilla game and BG2EE.
  5. Many Bioware NPCs (Jaheira, Minsc, Korgan, Jan, Mazzy, Edwin, Aerie) are tweaked to become more attractive as choices for party members.
  6. Major content changes and additions:
    • the Avenger kit is completely redesigned and assigned to Jaheira;
    • a new druidic spell is added, complete with custom-made new animations;
    • a small tactical encounter is added to spice up the early stages of the game;
    • a major new encounter, Improved Demogorgon, is added to provide a satisfied finale to the Watcher's Keep adventure;
    • Necromancer's brand new spell, Recast Vital Energy, is toned down to stimulate a more balanced and diverse spell book configuration;
    • a lot of new graphics added to the game: creature animations and custom colouring, spell animations, portrait icons, new item graphics and so on;
  7. Major item-related changes and additions:
    • expanded randomizer program: four more items are added to random locations;
    • a couple of items moved to the new static locations, as well;
    • a third craftsman is introduced to provide services for the player's party;
    • 9 brand new item upgrades are added to the game;
    • various tweaks are introduced to existing in-game items;
    • a couple of existing items are tweaked to be equippable by the druid characters;
    • magical throwing axes and hammers can now be dual-wielded when in melee mode;
    • efforts made, both through item upgrades and item placement, to make scimitars, katanas and spears more attractive weapon choices.
Version 6.1
  1. New version of WeiDU compiler is adopted.
  2. Numerous patches introduced to allow for compatibility with BG2EE.

  5. Numerous bugs and exploits found in IAv6 are fixed.
  6. New spells and abilities are added to existing classes.
  7. Two new item upgrades, designed specifically for the new BG2EE NPCs, are added to the game.
  8. Readme files are updated to reflect the order of installation for BG2EE.
  9. A new section is added to the Fixes & Tweaks readme file, detailing bugs and issues that still may exist in the game.

Version 4.0
  1. Many more fixes and tweaks apllied to the original game.
  2. Many More tactical challenges.
  3. New quests and adventures as well as expanded strongholds.
  4. Many new additional encounters.
  5. Many more new types of monsters.
  6. Refinements applied to the tactical content of the previous versions of the mod.
  7. Radomization program applied to the location of some of the game's items. In this program, some of the game's original items which were too easily available have been removed from their original locations. Every time you start a new game, the items will be randomly distributed between a number of tough and improved enemies in the game, which means that each time you play the mod the items may be with different opponents and you won't be sure where to look for each of them.
  8. Polygonal scripting system: it's an advanced scripting method devised for and used in Improved Anvil. As a result, creatures can behave much more intelligently.
  9. Some of the items' prices in the shops are increased. Also, cheesy methods to make easy money (stealing items and selling them back) are blocked and will not work anymore.
  10. Some of the key items of the mod are made uncheatable and should only be gained or forged normally during the game.
  11. Some of the original game's overpowered items and spells are nerfed.
  12. A new ranger kit is added to the game.
